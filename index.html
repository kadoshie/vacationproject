OUR ROOMS

Sleeps up to 4 people, with 2 bedrooms. Master bedroom has a King size bed, master bath and a small closet. The second bedroom has 2 twin beds and its bathroom. They are best for a family or a group. It has a kitchen and a beautiful living area with a large stone wood fireplace. Curl up by the crackling fire with a good book, watch a movie or share stories together at the balcony as the sun sets. Superior Rooms

The Superior Room comprises of 1 Double bed or 2 Twin Beds, 2 Bedside Tables, a Desk & Chair.
This room is larger than the Standard Room and is therefore spacious enough for an additional bed for triple occupancy. (Triple Room).
The room is furnished with wall to wall carpeting, trendy furnishings and a balcony. Our ultramodern glass bathroom is equipped with hairdryer,
magnifying shaving and make up mirror as well as all the amenities you could possible need during your stay. A Complimentary Bottle of Wine, Fresh Fruit and Mineral Water, are provided on arrival. Electric current: 220 Volts. Smoking rooms are also available.
